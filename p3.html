Weedicide

Nastar (2,4-D Sodium Salt 80% WP) is used mainly as a post-emergent herbicides for control of broad leaved weeds in cereals, sugarcane, tea, wheat, rice, pastures, barley, turf, non-crop areas, sorghum, oats etc.

Atramine (ATRAZINE 50% W.P) is used for season-long pre and early postemergence weed control in corn, sorghum, and certain other crops.viz African oil palm, bananas, citrus groves, coffee, corn, pineapple, sorghum, sugarcane, corn hybrids, field corn and industrial weeds.
